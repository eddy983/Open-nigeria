About Us

Data is at the center of the digital development phase and
a cornerstone of the Government's transition strategy.

About-section

Open nigerian states is launched to help people find and access open government data, and assist policy publishers in data preservation which goes beyond being a repository of data. We aim, through the extensive use of charts and articles to make policy data meaniful and accessible for the public.

Good data can help to support informed decision management and ongoing quality enchancement. Departments are expected to combat theft and reduce duplication. Through embracing legal, responsive, creative and clear data solutions, we will create greater levels of trust with people while providing more cost effective, more personalized and customized initiatives to their needs.
